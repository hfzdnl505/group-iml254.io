Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is there an online delivery service available at EAU DÉ CAFE?
Yes! We provide online delivery through our website and selected delivery partners like GrabFood and FoodPanda. Just order, sit back, and enjoy!
2. Is the food served at EAU DÉ CAFE halal-certified?
Yes, all our food is halal-certified and prepared according to halal standards.
3. Are there any promotions or discounts available for regular customers?
We regularly offer promotions during festive seasons, weekends, and for loyal customers. Check our social media for the latest deals!
4. Will EAU DÉ CAFE introduce new items or seasonal menus in the future?
Yes, we always work on exciting seasonal menus and limited-time offers to keep things fresh and delicious. Stay tuned on our Instagram!
5. How many branches does EAU DÉ CAFE have, and where are they located?
Currently, EAU DÉ CAFE has 4 branches: Kedah (main branch), Ipoh, Kuala Lumpur, and Kelantan. More are coming soon!
6. What are the operating hours and weekly holidays?
We open every day from 4:00 PM until 2:00 AM. We are closed every Monday.
7. Can I host private events or parties at EAU DÉ CAFE?
Yes, we offer private event packages for birthdays, anniversaries, and corporate functions. Contact us for custom arrangements.
8. Do you provide vegetarian or vegan options on your menu?
Definitely! We offer a variety of vegetarian dishes and we're working on expanding our vegan options soon.
9. Is Wi-Fi available for customers inside the cafe?
Yes, free high-speed Wi-Fi is available for all customers — perfect for studying, working, or chilling.
10. Do I need to make a reservation or can I walk in?
Walk-ins are always welcome, but for larger groups or events, we recommend booking in advance to secure your spot.
