About EAU DÉ CAFE

Section Content
Vision Providing a unique and unforgettable cafe experience for every customer.
Mission & Objective Providing high-quality food and beverages, offering a comfortable and friendly environment, and delivering the best customer service.
History

Since January 2025, a cafe known as "eau de cafe" has been established in the middle of a busy city. With a vision to create a harmonious space between humans and nature, this cafe adopts the concept of "nature" in every aspect of its operation. From the interior design that uses natural materials such as wood and stone, to the use of soft lighting and greenery that adorns every corner, this cafe creates a calming and reconcile vibes.

Within a short journey, Cafe Alam Semula Jadi has attracted the attention of many local visitors and tourists. In May 2025, this cafe was recognized as one of the best cafes in the area. This excellence is not separated from the focus given to authentic Malay food and dishes. The menu offered contains a variety of traditional Malay dishes.

Apart from that, this cafe is also famous for its unique Malay-themed desserts and drinks. From traditional cakes such as ondeh-ondeh and kuih lapis to drinks such as teh tarik and bandung, each dish is prepared using high-quality ingredients.

The success of eau de cafe not only in its delicious food, but also in its commitment to supporting the environment. By adopting eco-friendly practices such as using recycled containers and reducing food waste, the cafe has become an example for other businesses in the food and beverage industry.

To this day, eau de cafe continues to strive to provide an unforgettable experience for every visitor, while appreciating the Malay cultural heritage and the beauty of nature.
