Our Logo Meaning

☕ The coffee bean cup in the middle symbolizes our focus on handcrafted coffee, freshness, and quality with every brew.
🧵 The folded fabric design with "Coffee and Western" written below the cup represents a warm blend of traditional café culture with a modern western twist — perfect for cozy vibes and delicious food.
🔠 Our cafe name on top proudly identifies EAU DÉ CAFE as a brand of elegance, taste, and comfort.
🔘 The dotted circle around the logo adds a modern vintage aesthetic, like the stitching on a coffee bag — showing detail, care, and artisanal value.
🎨 The light beige background reflects calmness, comfort, and cleanliness — just like the ambiance of our café.
📅 The “20/25” symbolizes our cafe's launch year (2025) and our aspiration to serve for at least 20 years and beyond.
